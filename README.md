## üìå 1. Project Idea & Executive Summary

The core objective is to leverage advanced **Data Analysis** to move beyond reactive maintenance by delivering a **Root Cause Analysis (RCA)** of textile manufacturing downtime. We will synthesize **operational, human resource, and maintenance quality data** to develop **Predictive Models** capable of forecasting equipment failure and informing strategic decisions related to resource allocation and maintenance optimization.

**Ultimately, this project aims to maximize machine productivity and avoid critical failures to enhance overall production efficiency and increase the plant's profitability.**

## üìÖ 3. Work Plan  

1. **Data Collection & Integration**  
   - Gathering operational and maintenance.  
   - Adjusting and aligning column structures to ensure consistency across all data sources.  
    

2. **Data Cleaning & Preparation**  
   - Handling missing values, outliers, and formatting inconsistencies.  
   - Applying normalization and data type standardization for better compatibility.  
   - Ensuring data validity and readiness for advanced analysis.  

3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**  
   - Identifying downtime trends, correlations, and performance bottlenecks.  
   - Building comparison visuals.  
   - Using **Python** and **Excel** for initial statistical exploration.  

4. **Visualization & Insights**  
   - Designing interactive dashboards across **Excel** and **Power BI** to highlight:  
     - Machine performance patterns  
     - Maintenance frequency  

5. **Predictive Modeling**  
   - Applying  predictive analysis to forecast downtime frequency.  
   - Performing **feature engineering** on maintenance logs and equipment data.  
   - Evaluating model accuracy and generating insights for optimization.  

6. **Documentation & Reporting**  
   - Compiling all analytical findings into structured visual and written reports.  
   - Explaining correlations, causes, and proposed actions for downtime reduction.  
   - Exporting visuals and models in standardized formats for review.  

7. **Final Presentation & Delivery**  
   - Preparing an executive presentation summarizing the entire workflow.  
   - Uploading dashboards, datasets, and project documentation to GitHub & Google Drive.
